ADMISSION ELIGIBILITY RULES — IDP Knowledge Base
=================================================

GENERAL ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS
----------------------------------
1. All applicants must have passed 10th grade (Class X / SSC / Matriculation) from a recognised board.
2. All applicants must have passed 12th grade (Class XII / HSC / Intermediate / Plus Two) from a recognised board.
3. A valid government-issued photo ID is mandatory. Aadhar card is the accepted identity document.
4. All submitted documents must be original or certified true copies.
5. Name on all documents must match. Minor spelling variations are acceptable but must be flagged for manual review.
6. Date of Birth must be identical across all submitted documents.

MINIMUM PERCENTAGE REQUIREMENTS BY COURSE
-------------------------------------------
B.Tech (Bachelor of Technology):
  - 10th Minimum: 35%
  - 12th Minimum: 45% in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics (PCM stream)
  - Stream Required: Science with PCM

B.Sc (Bachelor of Science):
  - 10th Minimum: 35%
  - 12th Minimum: 45% in Science stream
  - Stream Required: Science

B.Com (Bachelor of Commerce):
  - 10th Minimum: 35%
  - 12th Minimum: 45% in Commerce or Arts stream
  - Stream Required: Commerce or Arts

BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration):
  - 10th Minimum: 35%
  - 12th Minimum: 45% in any stream
  - Stream Required: Any

BA (Bachelor of Arts):
  - 10th Minimum: 35%
  - 12th Minimum: 40% in any stream
  - Stream Required: Any

MBA (Master of Business Administration):
  - Graduation Minimum: 50% in any discipline
  - Work experience preferred but not mandatory

M.Tech (Master of Technology):
  - B.Tech/B.E. Minimum: 55% in relevant engineering discipline
  - GATE score preferred

SPECIAL CASES AND EXCEPTIONS
------------------------------
1. SC/ST/OBC candidates: 5% relaxation in minimum percentage for all courses.
2. Differently-abled candidates: 5% relaxation in minimum percentage for all courses.
3. Sports quota: 5% relaxation subject to state-level or national-level participation certificate.
4. NRI/Foreign nationals: Must submit equivalent qualification certificates with notarised translation.
5. Gap year: Applicants with gap year must submit a self-declaration affidavit.

DOCUMENT AUTHENTICITY REQUIREMENTS
------------------------------------
1. All marksheets must carry the official board seal/stamp.
2. Roll number must be present on marksheets.
3. Aadhar card must show the applicant's current photograph.
4. Documents with corrections/overwriting must be countersigned by issuing authority.
5. Photocopies must be self-attested by the applicant.
